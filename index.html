How It Works

Find Clues. Solve Riddles. Win Sats.

Whether you're exploring digital worlds or physical locations, Sats are hidden everywhere. Follow the clues, solve the puzzles, and collect Bitcoin as your prize.

  • A Bitcoin wallet loaded with millions of sats has been created using a 12-word BIP-39 seed phrase.
  • Each of the 12 words has been distributed to 12 trusted partners.
  • These partners will randomly reveal their words on Twitter or Nostr using #SatHunterz.
  • As more words go public, your chances of cracking the wallet grow —faster guesses, shorter brute force time.
  • Be the first to reconstruct the full seed phrase to unlock the wallet and claim the sats.
  • Track the hunt live on Twitter with #SatHunterz — the countdown is on.

FAQ

When does Sat Hunterz begin and how long will it run?

Check the popup window in the bottom-left corner for active Sat Hunterz games. More online and real-world hunts are coming soon—stay tuned!

Who are the partners securing the Sat Hunterz wallet?

Each game features a new set of partners helping to secure one of the 12 seed words. You can see the current partners listed at the top of our homepage.

Why are you giving away free sats?

Because Bitcoin should be fun. Sat Hunterz is designed to bring Bitcoiners together to team up, explore, and chase real value in a gamified way.

What if no one finds the wallet backup?

If the wallet isn’t found by the end of the game, we’ll start dropping hints on our Twitter—like which word corresponds to which number in the seed phrase.

Where can I find updates?

Follow us on Twitter and keep checking this website for the latest clues, partner reveals, and news.

How can I contact the team?

Send us a message on Twitter or email us at hello@sathunterz.com — we’d love to hear from you.
